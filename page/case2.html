Customer and Revenue Analysis

Modal title

2015 is the first year, so it does't have previous data.

  • Total revenue for the current year: Sum of the revenue this year.
  • New Customer Revenue: Sum of the revenue from new customers this year.
  • Existing customer growth: Revenue of existing customers for current year minus revenue of existing customers from the previous year.
  • Revenue lost from attrition: Sum of the revenue from lost customers this year.
  • Existing Customer Revenue Current Year: Sum of the revenue from old customers this year.
  • Existing Customer Revenue Prior Year: Sum of the revenue from old customers previous year.
  • Total Customers Current Year: Number of customers this year.
  • Total Customers Previous Year: Number of customers previous year.
  • New Customers: Customers that don't exist in previous year and exist in this year.
  • Lost Customers: Customers that exist in previous year and don't exist in this year.


    • From the plots, we found very strong customer mobility. They lost many customers every year, and attracted many new customers every year as well. Number of lost customers and number of new customers both increased. In addition, old(existing) customers have similar revenue in consecutive two years. Variation trend of different kinds of revenue is aligned with variation trend of corresponding customers.
